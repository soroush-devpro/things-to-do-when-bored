Bored? Try these tips and tricks to kill boredom, at home

If you're bored on the computer and trying to find something to check out in apps and the web, here are some ideas to just get you started:

  • Check YouTube
  • Watch some Netflix
  • Scroll through Pinterest for more ideas
  • Facebook and Twitter are great
  • View some funny videos on Instagram
  • Connect with friends on Snapchat
  • Write a story in Microsoft Word
  • Make a PowerPoint presentation
  • Make a graph in Excel
  • Play some Minecraft
  • Check your email
  • Organize your files
  • Chat with friends on Zoom or Discord
  • If you have work to finish, do it first thing
  • Try not to be on for more than 7 hours
  • Once your finish, do other things and then rinse and repeat, if you want
